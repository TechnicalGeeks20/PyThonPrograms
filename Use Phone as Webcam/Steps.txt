1. Download the app , Link Below 
	https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pas.webcam

2. open app and Click Start Server in the end

3. Video will get start in your phone

4. To Connect with your Python Program copy the IP address shown on you phone and Paste in the following function
	
	cap = cv2.VideoCapture("http://{Your:IP:ADDRESS}/video")

	(Make sure to include /video at the end)

5. You can visit the URL to change your camera settings like switching between front and back.
